About Me

Research bringing AI and human activity together through innovative technology

Research

I'm a researcher at Technische Hochschule Lübeck focused on developing innovative sensor technology, especially smart footwear, to create robust algorithms for human activity recognition. My work pushes the boundaries of how AI can understand and interpret human movement patterns in real-world environments.

Expertise

My research interests include real-time activity recognition with multisensory systems, surface identification using capacitive sensing, and applications in healthcare, sports, and autonomous systems. I specialize in bringing AI to edge devices like smart footwear to enable efficient on-device processing.

Beyond Research

When I'm not immersed in research, I find balance through music and creative expression. Playing guitar provides a creative counterpoint to my analytical work, helping me maintain creativity and perspective in my technical pursuits.
